HOW TO PLAY
🐺 WEREWOLF
Catch all hiders before the timer runs out. Use the SCAN button to detect nearby prey. After 20 seconds the lights go out — your vision is much better in darkness.
🏃 HIDER
Survive until the timer ends. Walk near glowing blue circles to find hiding spots. Press HIDE to become invisible. Avoid the werewolf — especially in the dark.
🕹 CONTROLS
Left joystick to move · SCAN button (wolf only) · HIDE button (hiders) · WASD keys on desktop · Space = scan
